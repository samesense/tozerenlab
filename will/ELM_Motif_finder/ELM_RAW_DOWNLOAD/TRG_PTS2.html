 Abstract

PTS2, peroxisomal targeting signal 2, is one of two peroxisomal import
signals which direct proteins into the lumen (matrix) of the
peroxisome. (The other being PTS1.) A third signal is used to target
peroxisomal membrane proteins. PTS2 is found less often than PTS1 and has been lost in the lineage leading to Ceanorhabditis elegans (and perhaps other nematodes too).
