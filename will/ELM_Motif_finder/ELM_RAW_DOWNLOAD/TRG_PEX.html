 Abstract

This restricted motif is involved in import of peroxisomal matrix proteins. The multidomain protein Pex5p is the receptor for the PTS1 targeting signal. It contains several WXXXY|F repeats. These interact with two other proteins involved in matrix protein import, Pex13p and Pex14p. Pex13p is an integral membrane protein with a C-terminal SH3 domain that makes an atypical interaction with WXXXY|F.
